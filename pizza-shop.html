MO'S PIZZA

Grab a delicious, authentic Italian meal at Mo's Pizza. Choose from a variety of pizzas, pastas, and fresh salads, all made with high-quality ingredients. Perfect for families and groups looking for a tasty break during their zoo adventure!

They serve the most delicious thick-crust pizzas and delicious pastas.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

Mo's Pizza is located in the central area of the zoo, near the Amphitheatre (E21) and the Insect House (E23). It is easily accessible from the main pathways, making it a convenient stop for visitors looking to refuel during their zoo visit.
