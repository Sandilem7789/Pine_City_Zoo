THE LOST FOREST

Relax and enjoy a picnic with your family and friends in this shady picnic spot by the edge of the lake.

The Lost Forest is a serene and beautiful area within the zoo, perfect for a peaceful retreat. You can take a leisurely stroll along the paths, enjoy the lush greenery, and have a picnic by the lake.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

The Lost Forest is located in the eastern part of the zoo, near the Monkey Trail (E22) and the Amphitheatre (E21). It is easily accessible from the main pathways, and there are signs to guide you to this tranquil spot.
