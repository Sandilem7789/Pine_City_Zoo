DINE @THE ZOO

Gourmet meals, a serene atmosphere, live music and good company - sometimes that's all you need.

Dine @The Zoo offers a unique dining experience with a menu that features gourmet dishes made from locally sourced ingredients. The restaurant is set in a beautiful outdoor setting, allowing you to enjoy your meal surrounded by nature. It's the perfect place for a special occasion or a relaxing meal after exploring the zoo.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

Dine @The Zoo is located in the central area of the zoo, near the Amphitheatre (E21) and the Insect House (E23). It is easily accessible from the main pathways, making it a convenient stop for visitors looking to enjoy a fine dining experience amidst the natural beauty of the zoo.
