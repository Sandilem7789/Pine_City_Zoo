INSECT HOUSE

The Insect House is home to one of the largest and most diverse insect collections in the world. Here you will see creepy crawlies from all over the world, in every shape and size.

Today's program:

  • 09h00am : Butterfly Release - watch butterflies take flight
  • 11h00am : Meet the Ants - a talk on ant colonies
  • 14h00pm : Creepy Crawlies - a guided tour of the insect house
  • 16h00pm : Insect Feeding Time - see how insects are fed
  • 18h00pm : Night of the Beetles - a special evening event

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

Insect House is at the eastern side of the zoo, next to the Monkey Trail.
